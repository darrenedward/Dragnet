# Code of Conduct

Dragnet is a community project. We are committed to making participation welcoming, respectful, and constructive for everyone.

Expected behavior includes:

- communicating with empathy and professionalism;
- assuming good intent while addressing problems directly;
- focusing criticism on code, proposals, and behavior rather than people;
- accepting responsible feedback and maintainer decisions.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, discrimination, threats, doxxing, sexualized attention, and deliberately disruptive conduct.

Report concerns to `darren-edward@hotmail.com`. Reports will be handled as privately as practical.
